Yongqi Li is a PHD student in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He received the B.E. degree in School of Computer Science and Technology from Shandong University, in 2019. His research interests include information retrieval and multimedia computing. Now he mainly focuses on text retrieval and conversational information seeking, such as generative retrieval, conversational QA, and multimodal conversational QA.
